Common Start (prepend to every prompt):

Analyze the provided annual report (including Annual Report, Management Discussion & Analysis, Notes to Accounts, Corporate Governance Report, Auditor's Report, AOC-1, Investor Presentation, and financial statements). Extract only information explicitly disclosed in the report. Quote relevant figures, dates, percentages, and page numbers wherever available. Present the output in a structured table format. If information is not available, state "Not Disclosed".

1. Business Overview
Analyze the annual report and extract:
- Line of business and nature of operations
- Main products and services offered
- Core business activities
- Principal revenue-generating activities
- Company overview and business model
Include management commentary describing the company's business and competitive positioning.

2. Business Segments
Analyze the annual report and extract:
- Operating segments reported by the company
- Business divisions and segment descriptions
- Revenue contribution by segment
- Profitability/performance by segment
- Geographical segments and regional revenue split
- Segment-wise growth trends and management commentary
Present segment information for the latest three years if disclosed.

3. Annual Financials (3 Years)
Revenue:
- Revenue from Operations
- Total Revenue / Total Income
- Net Sales / Turnover
- Other Income
- YoY growth %
Present both standalone and consolidated figures where available.

EBITDA:
- EBITDA / Operating Profit / PBDIT
- EBITDA Margin %
- Operating Margin %
- Key drivers of margin expansion or contraction
Include management explanations where disclosed.

PAT:
- Profit After Tax (PAT)
- Net Profit
- Profit attributable to shareholders
- Basic EPS
- Diluted EPS
- YoY PAT growth %
Include commentary on major profit drivers.

4. Quarterly Financials (Last 3 Quarters)
Extract:
- Revenue from Operations
- EBITDA / Operating Profit
- EBITDA Margin
- PAT / Net Profit
- EPS
- QoQ growth %
- YoY growth %
Summarize management commentary on quarterly performance.

5. Auditor Details
Auditor Name:
- Name of Statutory Auditor
- Audit Firm Registration Number (FRN)
- Auditor's tenure
- Type of audit opinion issued
- Key Audit Matters (KAMs)

Auditor Fees:
- Audit fees paid to statutory auditors
- Fees for tax, certification, advisory, and other services
- Total remuneration paid to auditors

Auditor Appointment:
- Auditor appointment/reappointment details
- AGM resolution details
- Term of appointment
- Effective dates
- Retirement and replacement details, if any

6. Subsidiaries, Associates & Joint Ventures
Extract:
- List of subsidiaries
- Associate companies
- Joint ventures
- Ownership percentages
- Country of incorporation
- Financial performance of subsidiaries
- New entities incorporated/acquired during the year
- Entities liquidated, merged, or divested

7. New Business Verticals & Growth Initiatives
Extract:
- New business verticals
- New product launches
- New service offerings
- Diversification initiatives
- Entry into new markets
- Emerging business opportunities
- Strategic investments supporting future growth

8. Latest Developments & Strategic Initiatives
Extract:
- Major milestones achieved during the year
- Strategic initiatives undertaken
- Key operational achievements
- Expansion plans
- Technology investments
- ESG and sustainability initiatives
- Significant announcements made by management

9. M&A / Divestments
Extract:
- Acquisitions
- Mergers
- Takeovers
- Amalgamations
- Slump sales
- Business transfers
- Divestments
- Asset sales
For each transaction provide:
- Target/company name
- Transaction value
- Date
- Strategic rationale
- Expected benefits

10. Capital Markets Activities
Extract:
- IPO
- FPO
- OFS
- Rights Issue
- QIP
- Preferential Allotment
- Warrants/Convertible Instruments
- Debt raising activities
Include:
- Amount raised
- Date
- Purpose of funds
- Utilization status

11. Operations & Business Outlook
Extract:
- Management outlook for the next 1–3 years
- Order book and new orders won
- Capacity utilization
- Capacity expansion plans
- Operational efficiency initiatives
- Fundraising plans
- Industry growth expectations

12. Risks & Headwinds
Extract all risks discussed in:
- Risk Management section
- MD&A
- Auditor observations
- Notes to Accounts

Categorize risks into:
- Macroeconomic risks
- Industry risks
- Regulatory risks
- Supply chain risks
- Technology risks
- Competition risks
- Financial risks
- ESG and sustainability risks

Universal Output Instruction:
Create a structured output with columns:
Category | Extracted Information | Financial Impact (if any) | Page Number | Source Section | Management Commentary.

Do not infer or estimate information not explicitly disclosed.
